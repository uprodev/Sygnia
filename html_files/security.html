OT SECURITY

As targeting of the industrial sector continues to intensify, the IT and OT environments of industrial enterprises are at ever-greater risk. It is increasingly important for heavy industry to align its OT Security strategy with the current threat landscape to protect its operational technology.

From proactive resilience enhancement to reactive incident response, Sygnia’s holistic OT security offering has been designed to enable enterprise-wide cybersecurity across the digital estate.
