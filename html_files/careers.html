Under Attack

JOIN THE TEAM BEHIND THE WORLD’S MOST CYBER-READY ORGANIZATIONS

The foremost global cyber readiness and response team is built with exceptional people. If you are passionate about cyber security, want to work with some of the best minds in the industry, and are committed to excellence and client impact, Sygnia may be the place for you.

Our culture

Sygnia is defined by a culture that embraces camaraderie and respect, encourages bold thinking and hard work, and adheres to the highest professional and ethical standards on behalf of our clients.

As a Sygnia employee, you can enjoy mentorship and teambuilding programs, access pro-bono and community initiatives, and share in our commitment to evangelizing technology best practices. We foster a dynamic and open-minded environment to ensure that together we can continue working at the edge of cyber.

Why choose Sygnia

Reinforced by an outstanding culture, Sygnia is the smart choice for motivated and ambitious cyber security professionals at all levels of experience. Here’s why:

Exposure

Gain opportunities to do important, cutting-edge work for global leaders of industry.

Development

Cultivate and grow your career with an organization that invests in its people.

Impact

Be a part of contributing to cyber wisdom, elevating the art of cyber security, and shaping our cyber future.
