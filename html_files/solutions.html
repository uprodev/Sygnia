CYBER READY AT EVERY STAGE OF YOUR SECURITY JOURNEY

Sygnia’s holistic, end-to-end solution set spans proactive and reactive cybersecurity. From executive-level strategic guidance to on-the-ground technical expertise, and across the security journey, everything we do is in service to our clients’ best defense.

SERVICES

The actions taken in the first 24 hours following a breach determine whether it is a cyber incident or a full-blown crisis. This truth drives our urgency to ensure our clients are cyber ready in times of crisis and calm. We are by their side to contain attacks at top speed and recover with as little business impact as possible. And we help them prepare for the threats that lie ahead, so their business is ready for anything that comes their way.

Technologies

Sygnia’s teams draw on deep technical expertise to work effectively with any IT or security stack, in any domain including cloud, application, CI/CD, OT, mobile, iOT, and traditional network infrastructure. Velocity XDR

Velocity XDR is a unified security detection and response platform designed for contextualizing and understanding masses of alerts that arrive from multiple, siloed systems. This proprietary technology offers unmatched speed of forensic collection, detection, triage, analysis and mitigation.
